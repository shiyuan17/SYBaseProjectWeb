| DEC-20260609-002 | 2026-06-09 | M2 specimen barcode binding semantics | A specimen registered without an explicit barcode remains `UNBOUND` with `barcode=null`; `POST /api/v1/specimens/{specimenId}/barcode-binding` is the first-write path for `targetBarcode`.
